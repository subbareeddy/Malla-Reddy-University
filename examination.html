Examination

The University will conduct examinations and confer the degrees and other academic distinctions on persons who fulfil the requisite qualifications/ conditions. The Examination Branch of the University, therefore, is the backbone of the University. The Examination Branch is headed by a Controller of Examinations. It works with the help of Additional Controllers, Computer Programmers, and Office Assistants. The Examination Branch conducts the Examinations, declares the results, and issues the relevant certificates to the student. It also maintains the personal and academic records of all the students admitted to various courses.

Examination Branch is well established with modern equipment and adequate experienced teaching and non-teaching staff for conducting examinations and publication of results. The functioning of the entire examination branch including the spot valuation is constantly monitored through state-of-the-art technology. Data storage and backup is protected through encryption. Certificates / Documents issued by the branch have many security features. The Examination Branch is continuously upgraded with the latest technology in various aspects.
